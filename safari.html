SAFARI

SAFARIS IN KENYA

Immerse yourself in a world of adventure at any of our three Safari destinations, each located in one of Kenya's prime National Parks or Conservancies. Marvel at the sunrise across the savannah or gaze at the starry nights skies. Feel your heart beat hasten as you watch the King of the jungle in all his majesty just footsteps away, venture to climb mountain like structures in community lands and explore the Masai and Samburu cultures as you connect to the absolute miracles of Mother Nature.

EXPERIENCE SAFARI IN MASAI MAARA GAME RESERVE

Sarova Mara Game Camp is located in the heart of the Masai Mara Game Reserve in South West Kenya. It is 260 km from Nairobi which is an hour flight from Nairobi and 20-minute drive from the airstrip or a 5 hour drive from Nairobi. The Camp is set on an elevation surrounded by two streams.

EXPERIENCE SAFARI IN SAROVA SHABAA NATIONAL RESERVE

Venture out into the raw unchartered terrains of the Shaba Reserve and the surrounding community lands for guided climbing experiences & nature trail walks. The vistas are a spectacle and each path has a story to tell.

EXPERIENCE SAFARI IN LAKE NAKURU NATIONAL PARK

Lake Nakuru National Park is home and sanctuary to both White Rhino and the endangered Black Rhino. Rhinos are an almost certain sighting, lions and leopards are often seen but rarely on open ground and the park is plentiful with buffalo, zebra, giraffe and birdlife.
