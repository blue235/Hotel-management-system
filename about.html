ABOUT US

SAROVA GROUP

From humble beginnings in 1972, our Founders built a dream imbibed with a deep respect for nature and community that lives in our DNA. Behind the company is a beautiful history of families, friendship and the power of will uniting to create Kenya's leading, independently owned, exceptional breed of hotels and resorts. As a historic part of Kenya's hospitality industry, over decades we have acquired a portfolio of exceptional properties. Sarova Hotels houses eight unique properties in prime locations, each harmoniously individual to its own history and environment, fortified by a vision to impact surrounding communities and build a sustainable future.
