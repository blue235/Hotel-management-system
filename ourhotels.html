OUR HOTELS

CITY

SAROVA STANTLEY

At Sarova Stanley, it's all about giving you an unforgettable luxury experience that feels effortless. From the moment you arrive, our friendly team is ready to make sure your stay is nothing but relaxing and enjoyable. Nestled in the heart of Nairobi's CBD, getting around is a breeze with easy access via the Nairobi Expressway to hotspots like JKIA Airport, Wilson Airport, KICC, UNEP Headquarters, and key government offices. Plus, you'll be just a short ride away from must-see attractions like the Nairobi National Park, Nairobi National Museum, Uhuru Park, and the best shopping destinations in town.

SAROVA PANAFRIC

Sarova Panafric is your gateway to world-class luxury right in the heart of Nairobi. Just 5-10 minutes from the CBD and 20 minutes from JKIA via the Expressway, it’s the perfect spot for an easy, immersive city experience. Plus, our peaceful gardens offer a Zen escape from the hustle and bustle. Whether you’re in town for business or leisure, you’ll love our top-tier facilities, delicious cuisine, and relaxing wellness options. Business travellers can take advantage of our premium conference spaces, while leisure guests can unwind at our poolside lounge bar or enjoy a meal at our classic restaurant.

SAROVA WOODLANDS HOTEL AND SPA NAKURU

Discover your perfect getaway at Woodlands Hotel & Spa, tucked away in Nakuru's upscale Milimani area, right next to the State House and just minutes from the city center. If you choose your trip here for a business meeting, a romantic escape, or a family adventure, you'll love how easy it is to explore nearby gems like Lake Nakuru National Park, Hell's Gate and of course, the vibrant night life. With 147 spacious, air-conditioned rooms, a stunning courtyard for those sunny days, and dining spots like Cinnamon Restaurant, The Leather Bar, and a beautiful poolside area, this is where luxury meets relaxation. Plus, don't miss our famous Sunday Brunch on the first Sunday of every month-a perfect hangout for friends and family.

SAROVA IMPERIAL HOOTEL IN KISUMU

Stay at Sarova Imperial Hotel Kisumu and experience the best of Kisumu's vibrant lakeside life! Located right in the heart of the city, just a 10-minute drive from Kisumu International Airport, you'll be close to all the must-see spots like the Kisumu Impala Sanctuary, Dunga Hill Camp, Hippo Point and the eclectic nightlife. With industrial-chic dècor and ambiance, our 95 ensuite rooms, including 3 signature suites, offer a cozy space to unwind, whether you're here for business or leisure. Stay active during your trip with access to our fully equipped fitness room, and enjoy top-tier service from a team that's all about making your stay unforgettable.

BEACH

SAROVA WHITESANDS BEACH RESORT AND SPA

Grab your sunscreen, pack your swimsuit, and get ready for sun, sea, and good vibes at Sarova Whitesands Beach Resort & Spa - Every day here feels like a holiday! Sarova Whitesands Beach Resort & Spa is where luxury meets adventure, whether you're here for business or fun. Nestled along the pristine shores of the Indian Ocean and surrounded by swaying palms & lush botanical gardens, it's the ultimate beach escape just 30 minutes from Mombasa's city centre and 21km from Moi International Airport. Whether you're chilling by the pool, enjoying al fresco dining, or getting pampered at our oceanfront spa, we've got everything you need for an epic getaway.

SAFARI

SAROVA MARA

A safari in Kenya is a once-in-a-lifetime adventure. You'll witness Africa's wildlife in its natural element firsthand. From the breathtaking annual migration of over 1 million wildebeest passing through the Masai Mara between July and October annually, to the rich culture and stunning landscapes - it's the perfect way to experience untouched Africa. Our camp is tucked right in the heart of the Masai Mara Game Reserve, offering an authentic African safari experience. Whether you're staying in one of our 20 Club Tents, 52 Deluxe Tents, or our 3 spacious Family Tents, each comes with modern comforts and en-suite facilities. Plus, some club tents feature jaw-dropping views of the park - wildlife sightings included!

SAROVA SHABA GAME LODGE

Tucked away in a serene oasis as the only lodge within Shaba National Reserve, Sarova Shaba Game Lodge gives you a luxury safari experience like no other. It’s your gateway to Kenya's untamed beauty, where stunning landscapes and rich wildlife set the stage for an unforgettable adventure. As the land of "Born Free" made famous by Joy and George Adamson’s story of Elsa the lioness, Shaba offers a sense of history and magic. Stay in one of our 85 spacious rooms, each with a view of the majestic Ewaso Nyiro River and calm, lush gardens fed by meandering natural streams all around. Whether you’re unwinding by the pool, enjoying fish foot therapy, dining under the stars, or exploring the wild with one of our expert guides, every moment is designed to leave you feeling relaxed and inspired.

SAROVA LION HILL GAME LOUNDGE

Discover the magic of Lake Nakuru National Park, home to the third-largest lake in Kenya, the endangered white rhino, the world’s biggest population of pink flamingos and unforgettable safari experiences. This is a safari destination like no other. Named after "The Lion Hill," on which the lodge sits, Sarova Lion Hill Lodge offers you more than just a stay. It’s your gateway to adventure & relaxation within the remarkable Lake Nakuru National Park. Wake up to breathtaking views from your private terrace, and enjoy the comforts of our classic chalet-style rooms. Whether you’re indulging in world-class cuisine, unwinding by the pool, or relaxing with an outdoor spa treatment, this is your chance to connect with nature in a truly special way.

SAROVA MAIYAN NANYUKI

Welcome to Sarova Maiyan Nanyuki, your getaway to luxury and adventure in one of Kenya’s most stunning holiday spots destinations. Just 20km from Nanyuki town, our resort offers 10 beautiful villas with a total inventory of 60 rooms spread across 113 acres of lush landscapes. Enjoy incredible views of Mt. Kenya, the Lolldaiga Hills, and the Aberdares - all best enjoyed experienced from your villa’s private rooftop pool lounge. Here, you'll find everything from refreshing dips in the pool and horseback riding at our equestrian center, to mini golf, an exotic bird aviary and a relaxing wellness spa. Our expansive grounds are perfect for team-building activities, unforgettable weddings and special events.
